Welcome to Hunza Valley

Nestled in the breathtaking Gilgit-Baltistan region, Hunza Valley is one of Pakistan's most famous tourist destinations. Surrounded by towering mountains, crystal-clear rivers, and charming villages, Hunza offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventure.

Rakaposhi Viewpoint

Rakaposhi Viewpoint is one of the most popular stops along the Karakoram Highway. It offers spectacular views of Rakaposhi Peak, which stands at 7,788 meters above sea level. Visitors can admire the snow-covered mountain while enjoying the fresh mountain air and stunning landscapes.

Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake is renowned for its vibrant turquoise waters and dramatic mountain surroundings. Created after a landslide in 2010, it has become one of the most visited attractions in northern Pakistan. Visitors can enjoy boating, jet skiing, and photography while taking in the breathtaking scenery.

Baltit Fort

Baltit Fort is a historic architectural masterpiece overlooking Karimabad. More than 700 years old, the fort served as the residence of the rulers of Hunza. Today, it functions as a museum where visitors can learn about the history, traditions, and culture of the region.
